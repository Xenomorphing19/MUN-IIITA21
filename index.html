About Us

IIITA-MUN is IIIT Allahabad’s flagship premier national student conference. Every year Effervescence, the cultural fest of IIIT Allahabad, has been the buzz of the town for its distinct penchant for displaying spectacular events, and IIITA-MUN is its key Flagship Event, hosted by SARASVA, IIIT Allahabad's Literary Society. With a footfall of 300+ in the offline version, IIITA-MUN'22 will be an online sought after opportunity for the globally connected India to take part in the annual cultural festival of IIITA and indulge in an intense diplomatic discussion to unravel the unpursued but potential paths and possibilities to the critical issues of the world that have kept diplomats scratching their heads over the years. The level of the IIITA Model United Nations conference has been increasing exponentially year after year- it has become one of the biggest MUNs in the Allahabad - Kanpur - Lucknow belt in the seven years since its inception. Our aim for this edition of IIITA MUN is not only to enhance the debate that takes place within the walls of the conference rooms but also to increase the reach of North India’s premier MUN.

Committees

This year, we present to you two committees:-


  1. United Nations General Assembly

    2. The United Nations General Assembly provides a unique forum for multilateral discussion of the entire spectrum of international issues covered by the Charter of the United Nations. This conventional and yet magnetic committee invites extensive participation with its comprehensive agendas, making it an ideal choice to begin MUN-ing with.

    Agenda : Human rights abuses during wartime with a focus on the Russia-Ukraine conflict.

    You can check out the Country Matrix here

  2. AIPPM Committee

    3. With more than 2000 registered political parties in India, the politics of the nation has been constantly changing. The AIPPM committee in MUN aims to encourage the political debate, discussion and deliberation that is not possible in parliament due to time constraints. With delegates representing personalities from the divergent group of Indian political parties, the main motive of the committee is to provide an unfiltered insight into national issues. While stepping into the shoes of assigned politician the AIPPM committee expects its representatives to be well versed, with their political party’s ideology, manifesto and beliefs which helps the representatives grasp the multi-layered processes that go behind policy-making and governance in India, providing them with a explicit experience to the hitches and hurdles that political parties accost in modern times. The AIPPM committee unlike other conventional MUN committee characterized by heated debates, cross talks, high levels of negotiations and political democracy echoes hopes of change and evolution!

    Agenda: Deliberation on the freedom of the press in India in the current political climate.

FAQs

What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations that aims to educate and encourage participants to discuss major issues concerning the world, topics in international relations, diplomacy and the United Nations agenda.

What are the dates of IIITA MUN?

IIITA MUN is a three day conference and will be held from 2nd to 4th of December.

What will be the manner of conduction of the conference?

The MUN conference this year will be conducted in offline mode in the IIIT Allahabad campus.

Is previous experience required to apply as a delegate?

To apply as a delegate no previous experience is required.

What awards will be provided at IIITA-MUN this year?

The awards will be presented at the discretion of the chairs of each committee and will include one Best Delegate, one High Commendation and two Special Mentions per committee.
