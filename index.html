About Us

IIITA-MUN is IIIT Allahabad’s flagship premier national student conference. Every year Effervescence, the cultural fest of IIIT Allahabad, has been the buzz of the town for its distinct penchant for displaying spectacular events, and IIITA-MUN is its key Flagship Event, hosted by SARASVA, IIIT Allahabad's Literary Society. With a footfall of 300+ in the offline version, IIITA-MUN'21 will be an online sought after opportunity for the globally connected India to take part in the annual cultural festival of IIITA and indulge in an intense diplomatic discussion to unravel the unpursued but potential paths and possibilities to the critical issues of the world that have kept diplomats scratching their heads over the years. The level of the IIITA Model United Nations conference has been increasing exponentially year after year- it has become one of the biggest MUNs in the Allahabad - Kanpur - Lucknow belt in the six years since its inception. Our aim for this edition of IIITA MUN is not only to enhance the debate that takes place within the walls of the conference rooms but also to increase the reach of North India’s premier MUN.

FAQs

What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations that aims to educate and encourage participants to discuss major issues concerning the world, topics in international relations, diplomacy and the United Nations agenda.

What are the dates of IIITA MUN?

IIITA MUN is a three day conference and will be held from 24th to 26th of December.

What will be the manner of conduction of the conference?

Due to the COVID-19 crisis, the MUN conference this year will be conducted in an online manner.

Is previous experience required to apply as a delegate?

To apply as a delegate no previous experience is required.

What awards will be provided at IIITA-MUN this year?

The awards will be presented at the discretion of the chairs of each committee and will include one Best Delegate and two Verbal Mentions per committee.
